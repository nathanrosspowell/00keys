Vault 13

Nuclear Data Green - SA Row 3

Groub Buy Completed

Design

Vault 13 is one of a series of fallout shelters developed by Vault-Tec in southern California beneath Mt. Whitney. It is located west of Shady Sands and Vault 15, and east of Mariposa Military Base. It was the home of the Vault Dweller.

Colorway

  • Nuclear Data Green

    This colorway matches the Nuclear Data keyset designed by samwisekoi and matt3o. It is inspired by the Nuclear Data Terminals and specifically the ND6600, an X-Ray Analysis System by Nuclear Data Inc.

    White on green are the base colors for the alpha and modifier keys.
